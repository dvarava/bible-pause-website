Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2025

1. Introduction

Bible Pause ("we", "our", or "the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Bible Pause is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • App usage data: We store your prayer session history, streaks, mood check-ins, and locked app selections locally on your device.
  • No personal accounts: Bible Pause does not require you to create an account or provide personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.
  • No analytics or tracking: We do not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

3. How We Use Your Information

All data generated by Bible Pause stays on your device and is used solely to provide the App's functionality, such as tracking your prayer streak and displaying your journey statistics.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device using secure on-device storage. We do not transmit your data to any external server. If you delete the App, all associated data is permanently removed.

5. Third-Party Services

Bible Pause does not share, sell, or disclose your personal information to third parties. The App does not integrate with any third-party data collection services.

6. Screen Time & App Blocking

Bible Pause uses Apple's Screen Time API (Family Controls / Device Activity) to block selected apps. The selection of which apps to block is stored locally and is never transmitted off your device. Apple's APIs handle this functionality in a privacy-preserving manner.

7. Children's Privacy

Bible Pause does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at bible-pause@gmail.com.
